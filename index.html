Frequently Asked Questions

What is CFD?

CFD is a platform where coders and developers connect and collaborate to find jobs and internships.

How does it work?

CFD connects coders with companies hiring for their skills. Companies post job openings or internships, and coders can apply directly.

Is it free?

CFD is free for coders to use. Companies may have fees for job or internship postings.

How can I post an internship?

Sign up and create an internship listing from your dashboard.
